Evan Bloom
This application was created by Evan Bloom. Evan Bloom is an Insight Data Science Fellow. Evan is interested in data science, analytics, economics, and helping people make better decisions with data. He is also a big fan of the San Francisco Giants.

Evan is completing a Ph.D. in Policy Analysis from the Pardee RAND Graduate School and most recently worked as an Assistant Policy Analyst at the RAND Corporation His research uses simulation modeling, computational experiments, statics and data-mining, and interactive visualization to share policy insights in policy contexts with deeply uncertain conditions. He has used these tools to help planners at the World Bank, U.S. Bureau of Reclamation, California Department of Water Resources, and the Metropolitan Water District adapt to climate change. Evan has also used similar methods to assist the L.A. Department of Health as they allocate resources among many interventions to prevent the spread of HIV. You can read more about Evan's research .
